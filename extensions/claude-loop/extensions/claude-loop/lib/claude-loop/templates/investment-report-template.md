# Investment Research Report Template

This template defines the structure for investment research reports generated by the Research Loop system.

**IMPORTANT:** Investment reports have MANDATORY sections that must always be included:
- Risk Assessment
- Full Disclaimer

---

## Template Variables

Standard variables (same as general report):
- `{{question}}` - The investment research question/subject
- `{{timestamp}}` - Report generation timestamp (UTC)
- `{{source_count}}` - Number of sources used
- `{{confidence_score}}` - Overall confidence score (0-100)
- `{{confidence_level}}` - Human-readable confidence level
- `{{findings}}` - List of key findings
- `{{gaps}}` - List of identified gaps
- `{{conflicts}}` - List of conflicts
- `{{sources}}` - List of source dictionaries

Investment-specific variables:
- `{{fundamental_findings}}` - Findings related to fundamentals
- `{{technical_findings}}` - Findings related to technicals
- `{{risk_findings}}` - Findings related to risks
- `{{bull_case}}` - Optimistic scenario description
- `{{base_case}}` - Expected scenario description
- `{{bear_case}}` - Pessimistic scenario description

### Standard Risk Factors

The following risks apply to most investments:

- **Market Risk**: General market conditions may impact performance
- **Liquidity Risk**: May not be able to exit position at desired price
- **Information Risk**: Analysis based on potentially incomplete or inaccurate data
- **Timing Risk**: Entry/exit timing significantly impacts returns
- **Concentration Risk**: Single-position exposure increases volatility
- **Currency Risk**: Exchange rate fluctuations may impact returns (if applicable)
- **Regulatory Risk**: Changes in regulations may impact the investment
